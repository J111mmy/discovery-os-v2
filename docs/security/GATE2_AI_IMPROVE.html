Market context

Construction projects in the UK and Ireland consistently run over schedule due to procurement delays. Material lead times have extended by 40–60% since 2021, and most site teams are still tracking submissions and deliveries in spreadsheets. The gap between what the programme says and what the site receives is invisible — until it's critical.

Target buyer

Mid-market construction PMs running projects of £5M–£100M, managing 3–8 concurrent contracts. They care about programme certainty above everything else. They don't buy software — they buy confidence that the programme won't blow out.

Core positioning statement

Veyor Procurement is purpose-built for construction and infrastructure project managers who need real-time visibility into material procurement timelines, submittal workflows, and on-site delivery scheduling — enabling them to proactively identify delays before they impact project completion dates, rather than discovering schedule risk only after it has already cascaded through the programme.
